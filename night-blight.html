Noctis Rubiginem

The Night Blight

Scientific Name: Noctis Rubiginem

The Night Blight is a small subterranean entity capable of burrowing through nearly any terrain, including soil, stone, and man-made materials. Despite its sharp claws and compact muscle structure, it poses little threat to humans.

Due to its natural digging behavior, the Night Blight has been used for pest control and agricultural soil aeration. Its primary predator is Entia Spiritus Vorax.
